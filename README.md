# Sales-Marketing-Analytics-Dashboard
To design and develop an interactive Power BI Sales &amp; Marketing Dashboard that provides insights into product performance, profit distribution, regional sales trends, customer behaviour, and discount impact. The objective is to enable data-driven decision-making for sales, marketing, and inventory management.
#  Project Overview

This project analyzes multi-year sales performance across categories, subcategories, regions, and customer segments. Using Power BI, the dashboard delivers real-time KPIs, trend analysis, top/bottom product insights, profit vs. discount behaviour, and region-wise profitability.

The report supports strategic business decisions by highlighting sales drivers, low-performing segments, and improvement opportunities.

#  Business Tasks / Key Questions Answered

Which categories and subcategories are generating the highest sales & profit?

Which products or regions are underperforming?

What is the relationship between discounts and profitability?

Which states/regions contribute the most to total revenue?

How do sales vary monthly and yearly (trend analysis)?

Which customer segments and products drive business peaks?

What actions can improve profitability and optimize inventory?

#   Key Insights

Technology category drives the highest revenue, mainly Phones & Accessories.

West & East regions contribute maximum sales.

High discounts reduce profits, especially in Office Supplies.

Chairs, Copiers, Phones are top profit-generating products.

Labels & Fasteners show consistently low performance.

Sales peak in Q2 & Q3, indicating seasonal buying trends.

# Recommendations

Reduce discounts on categories where profit is dropping.

Promote high-growth regions (West & East) with premium offerings.

Improve marketing strategies for low-performing subcategories.

Optimize inventory for high-demand products like Phones & Storage.

Boost Furniture category through selective discounting on premium items.
